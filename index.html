About

MixPose is a streaming platform to empower fitness professionals, yoga instructors and dance teachers through power of AI.

The idea behind MixPose is so that fitness professionals and yoga teachers can choose to teach anywhere they feel comfortable, such as beaches, waterfall, outdoors or their own home. And users can watch the stream in the comfort of their own TV.

With MixPose, yoga and fitness professionals can teach classes via streaming platform, which would generate income for them.

Mixpose also enables instructions from many places like India and Nepal, where yoga was born. Yogis are able to teach live classes from India and dance teachers are able to instruct from Latin America.
